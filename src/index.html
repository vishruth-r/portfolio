Carriage Cuisines

Carriage Cuisines is a Flutter mobile app with a Node.js backend that simplifies dining during train journeys. Users can log in, enter their train details and station, discover nearby restaurants using Google Maps, explore menus, place orders, and track order statuses, providing a convenient and enjoyable culinary experience while traveling.

Kavach National Hackathon

Our award-winning Content Moderation Browser Extension is the result of our team's collaborative efforts during the Kavach Hackathon. It addresses the critical issue of explicit content on the internet, offering a comprehensive solution to enhance online safety. Our extension, powered by machine learning models and a robust backend, efficiently classifies images, videos, and text for obscenity, blurs explicit visual content, flags inappropriate text, and integrates user feedback. The project showcases our commitment to promoting a safer digital environment.

MediCare App

Medicare is a healthcare management application, where a Flutter front-end communicates seamlessly with a Python backend. Leveraging a SQL database, the app enables users to book doctor appointments, purchase prescription medications, and manage their appointment history, all while maintaining data security and a smooth user experience. The Python backend efficiently handles data processing, appointment scheduling, and prescription management, ensuring a reliable and user-centric healthcare solution.
